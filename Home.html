BUDDO SECONDARY SCHOOL

Buddo Secondary School was founded by Mr. Muwonge Lawrence Matovu and started operations in February 1991 as a mixed, day and boarding secondary school for both O’ and A’ Level. This school is managed by a board of directors, who are experienced educationists and a competent board of governors.
